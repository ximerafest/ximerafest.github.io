XIMERA

￼ XIMERA is an inter-collegiate management symposium which attracts participants from leading B-Schools all over India. This year XIMERA promises to be bigger and better and hopes to trigger a fresh thought-process among the participants by equipping them with skills to re-look at industry practices with innovative ideas.

￼ With 3 Core events, 4 Specialization events and 2 Niche events our 2-day fest is designed to ignite the spark in students, to initiate actions and finally inspire the whole world.

THEME

Civilizations Around the World

￼ Many civilizations ruled the earth showing humanity the way forward. These civilizations are all different and diverse in nature. Management is also diverse with so many specializations. We at XIME are diverse too, hailing from all parts of the country. Celebrating these 25 years of our diversity, we present to you XIMERA ‘15!

XIME

￼ One of the leading private b-schools in India, Xavier Institute of Management & Entrepreneurship (XIME) has locations in Bangalore and Cochin, with a third one on the cards in Chennai. Found with a vision of imparting business education while pursuing high quality standards in teaching, training, research and consultancy, XIME has remained accountable to its stakeholders, through a process of continuous improvement. Over the past 21 years of its existence, it has gained the reputation of giving the business fraternity some of the eminent, capable and morally responsible business leaders who have steered their organisations to shores of success time and again.
